## What Went Wrong

1. **Chased the trade** - Entered after price already dropped from highs
2. **Entered in the lower third** - For shorts, you want to sell near resistance, not support
3. **Limited downside** - TPs are compressed into a tight range near support
4. **Large upside risk** - Price has 14% room to run against you before hitting range high

---

## Lessons Learned

### For SHORT trades:
- **Entry should be in the upper 60-70% of the range** (near resistance)
- Wait for price to test resistance and show rejection
- Don't short after a significant drop - you're selling the bottom

### The Right Setup Would Look Like:
